## What is the Toolkit?

CORE's Meta Agent has access to a powerful toolkit of integrations that enable it to take actions across your connected apps. From creating GitHub issues to sending Slack messages, managing Linear tasks to scheduling calendar events, your AI assistant can do it all through a unified interface.
