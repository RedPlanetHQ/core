## Cloud Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Dashboard">
    Visit [app.getcore.me](https://app.getcore.me) and create your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Gmail and Calendar">
    Grant CORE access to your Gmail (optional) and Google Calendar. CORE scans them to learn about you so your digital brain starts with real context from day one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Your Summary">
    Head to the **Document** section in your dashboard. CORE generates a summary of what it learned about you. This is the foundation of your digital brain.
  </Step>
</Steps>
